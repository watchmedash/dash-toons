Star Wars - The Mandalorian

Description: This is the first live-action drama series from Star Wars that follows Episode 6/The Return of the Jedi. 5 years after Darth Vader's death, peace has not yet arrived in the galaxy, and the remnants of the Empire have been behind the scenes. The lone bounty hunter “Mandalorian”, who survives on his own path in such a chaotic era meets the orphan “The Child” with mysterious power, and gets involved in an epic battle aimed at from all over the galaxy.
